LOAN PROGRAM

One payment instead of five. If the math works.

Roll credit cards, car notes, and other debt into your mortgage. I run the full comparison.

Overview

Credit cards, car loans, student debt, medical bills. If you're sitting on home equity and making five different payments at five different rates, consolidating them into your mortgage can simplify your life and reduce your total monthly outflow.

But here's the honest part: spreading short-term debt over a long mortgage term means you might pay more total interest even though your monthly payment drops. That tradeoff matters, and I make sure you see both numbers.

This isn't about hiding debt. It's about restructuring it intentionally, with full visibility into the long-term cost. I run the comparison and let you decide.

Who it's for
Homeowners with equity carrying high-interest debt across multiple accounts.
How it works
Cash-out refinance used to pay off credit cards, car loans, student loans, medical bills.

Lower monthly payment but potentially more total interest over time. I show you both numbers.

How I Handle This

I list out every debt you want to consolidate — balance, rate, minimum payment, and remaining term. Then I calculate what your new mortgage payment would be with those balances rolled in. You see the monthly savings AND the total interest comparison.

If the math works, we move. If it doesn't, I'll tell you and we explore other options.

Questions I Get

Will I actually save money?

Monthly? Almost always. Over the life of the loan? Depends. Spreading a five-year car note over a mortgage term increases total interest. I show you both numbers so you can make an informed decision.

What debts can I consolidate?

Anything you're making payments on — credit cards, auto loans, student loans, medical bills, personal loans. I'll help you prioritize which ones make sense to roll in.

What if I run the cards back up?

Then you've made your situation worse, not better. I'll be honest about this upfront. Consolidation is a tool, not a magic fix. It works when you use it with discipline.

Drowning in payments?

Send me your debt details and I'll run the consolidation comparison. Full transparency on monthly savings AND total cost.
