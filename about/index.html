Kellibrooke has been around for decades. It was a family company long before I took it over. Built, run, and eventually shelved for twenty years. I reopened it in 2020 under the same name and the same entity, continuing my mother's legacy.

I came up in this business learning how to process loans myself. Not how to sell them. How to build them.

Reading tax returns line by line. Reading lender guidelines the way underwriters read them. Structuring files so they don't fall apart in review. Negotiating with lenders when they push back. That's why I don't hand your file to a processor. I am the processor. I built the loan, I know every page in it, and I can defend every number in it when it matters.

How Kellibrooke works

When you call Kellibrooke, you get me. Not a receptionist, not a junior associate, not an automated system. I take the application, I build the file, I talk to the underwriter, I manage the timeline, and I show up at closing knowing every detail because I touched every piece of it. Something goes sideways at 9pm on a Thursday, I'm the one who picks up.

The files I built this business on are the ones other lenders won't touch. Self-employed borrowers whose tax returns don't tell the real story. Investors structuring DSCR loans across multiple properties. 1099 workers with five income streams and no W-2. People who got turned down somewhere else and were told to come back in a year. Those files take skill. That's why Kellibrooke exists.

Your file takes skill. Most lenders don't have that skill. I do.

You do not pay my fee. The lender does. No processing fees, no admin fees, no broker charges on your side of the table. I tell every client this upfront because it changes the math on whether a broker makes sense. It almost always does.

The Difference

How I work differently.

Other lenders quote you one thing and close on another. I don't do that.

One person, start to finish.

No hand-off to a processor you've never met. No "your loan officer is unavailable." I handle your file personally from pre-approval to closing, and I answer my phone.

I shop your loan for you.

A bank gives you their rate. That's it. I submit your profile across my entire lender network and bring you back the best option. Different lenders price different files differently. That's the whole point of using a broker.

You don't pay my fee.

My compensation comes from the lender, not from you. No processing fees, no admin fees, no junk charges that show up on your closing disclosure that weren't on your original estimate.

I'll tell you the truth.

If your bank already has the best deal, I'll say so. If your credit needs six more months of work before you should apply, I'll say that too. I'd rather lose a deal today and earn your trust for the next ten years.
