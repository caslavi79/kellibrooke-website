Overview

Life happens. Bankruptcy, foreclosure, short sale, collections, a rough stretch that left marks on your credit. These events don't permanently disqualify you from homeownership. Every program has specific waiting periods, and once you're past them, you're eligible.

The waiting periods vary by event type and by program. Some programs have shorter periods than others. Some allow exceptions for extenuating circumstances — job loss, medical emergency, divorce. I know every timeline for every program.

If you're past the waiting period, I can get you into a loan. If you're not there yet, I'll tell you exactly what to do and when to come back. Either way, you get a clear answer and a concrete plan.

How I Handle This

I start with the facts — what happened, when it happened, and what your credit looks like now. Then I check every program's waiting period timeline to determine your earliest eligibility.

If you're eligible now, we move forward. If you're not, I give you a specific plan — credit score targets, documentation to prepare, and a date to come back. No vague advice.

Questions I Get

How long do I have to wait after bankruptcy?

It depends on the type — Chapter 7 and Chapter 13 have different timelines, and each program (conventional, FHA, VA, Non-QM) has its own waiting period. I'll tell you exactly where you stand.

What about foreclosure?

Similar to bankruptcy — waiting periods vary by program. Some programs also consider whether there were extenuating circumstances. I check every option.

Can extenuating circumstances shorten the wait?

Yes, for some programs. If your credit event was caused by a documented hardship — job loss, medical emergency, divorce — the waiting period may be reduced. I help document the circumstances.

What should I do while I'm waiting?

Rebuild your credit strategically. I give you specific targets — score goals, account types to establish, utilization levels to maintain. So when your waiting period ends, you're ready to go immediately.

Past event holding you back?

Tell me what happened and when. I'll check every program's waiting period and give you a clear timeline — either we move now or I tell you exactly when.
