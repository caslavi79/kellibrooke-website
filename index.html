How It Works
Three steps. One person. Start to finish.
No hand-offs. No runaround. You talk to me the entire time.
01

Tell me your situation

Quick conversation or online form. I need to know what you are looking for, where you are buying, and your rough financial picture. This is not a full mortgage application yet. No SSN needed for an initial pre-approval estimate.
02

I shop my lender network

I submit your profile to my lenders and present you with competitive offers. You see every option. I explain the trade-offs. You pick what works.
03

Close fast, no surprises

I handle the paperwork, manage the timeline, and keep you updated at every step. My fastest clear-to-close was 5 days from application. Most loans close within 3 weeks.
