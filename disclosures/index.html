Broker Compensation

Kellibrooke Mortgage Partners, LLC operates on a lender-paid compensation model. The borrower does not pay the broker's fee. Compensation is paid by the lender at closing.

No processing fees, no administrative fees, and no broker charges are assessed to the borrower. All closing costs, lender fees, and third-party fees will be disclosed in your Loan Estimate and Closing Disclosure as required by the TILA-RESPA Integrated Disclosure (TRID) rules.
