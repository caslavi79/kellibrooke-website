## BRAND VOICE RULES

Read these before writing a single word. Violate any of these and I'll send it back.

### Tone
- First person. Case is talking directly to the reader.
- Short sentences. Punchy. No corporate fluff.
- Specific numbers beat vague claims.
- If it sounds like a brochure, kill it.
- If you wouldn't say it to someone at a bar, don't write it.

### Do
- "You'll know exactly what you're paying before you sign anything."
- "Here's your rate. Here's your payment. Here's what you save vs. your bank."
- "Not ready to apply? No pressure. Start with a free rate check."

### Don't
- "We leverage our robust lending platform to deliver world-class service tailored to your unique financial journey."
- "At Kellibrooke, we are passionate about helping families achieve their homeownership dreams."
- "Our team of experienced professionals is committed to excellence."

### Typography
- Display font: DM Serif Display (headings, hero, pull quotes)
- Body font: Outfit (paragraphs, nav, buttons, labels)
- Section labels: 10px, uppercase, 4px letter-spacing, gold

### Color Usage (for content, not code)
- Cream (#f8f5ef) is the ground. 85%+ of every page.
- Forest green (#1a3a2a) is headlines and card titles.
- Gold (#c9a84c) is accent — CTAs, links, highlights. Never large surfaces.
- Bark (#3d2b1f) is body text.

## COMPLIANCE RULES — CRITICAL

### NEVER use these (Reg Z trigger terms) without full TILA disclosures:
- Specific interest rates: "6.1%", "5.99%", "rates starting at 6%"
- Specific down payments: "3% down", "3.5% down", "$10,000 down"
- Specific monthly payments: "$2,800/month"
- Specific loan terms: "30-year fixed", "15-year mortgage"
- Specific finance charges: "$500 total cost", "2 points"
- Credit score minimums tied to programs: "580 credit score for FHA"

### SAFE language (use freely):
- "Competitive rates" / "Low rates"
- "Affordable payments" / "Flexible terms"
- "Low down payment options available"
- "Fast closings" / "Apply today"
- "Contact me for current details"
- "Flexible credit requirements"
- "Zero down" for VA (program feature, not trigger)
- "No PMI" for VA (program feature)
- Conforming loan limit ($832,750) is informational

### Every page MUST have in footer:
- Company name: Kellibrooke Mortgage Partners, LLC
- NMLS #2022197
- Individual NMLS #2025459
- Equal Housing Opportunity (NOT "Equal Housing Lender")
- NMLS Consumer Access link
- Texas SML complaint notice (ALL CAPS)

### Lender-paid compensation:
- CORRECT: "You do not pay my fee. The lender does."
- NEVER: "free", "no cost mortgage", implying borrower pays broker

### Stat claims:
- "1,400+ loans closed by Kellibrooke" (must include "by Kellibrooke")
- "5 days fastest clear-to-close" (must say "clear-to-close", not "closing")
